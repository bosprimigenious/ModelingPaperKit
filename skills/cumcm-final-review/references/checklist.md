# CUMCM Final Review Checklist

Critical:

- Build fails or final PDF missing.
- Electronic PDF includes paper-only identity pages.
- Required AI-use/source disclosure is false or missing.
- Identity information leaks into electronic submission.

Warning:

- Results do not directly answer a subproblem.
- Figure/table is unreferenced or unreadable.
- Appendix file list is stale.
- Missing validation or sensitivity analysis.

Info:

- Minor wording, caption, precision, or formatting improvements.
- Optional reproducibility cleanup.
