# CUMCM Paper Structure Checklist

- Abstract states methods and quantitative results, not only process.
- Problem restatement separates background, requirements, and subproblems.
- Assumptions are necessary, defensible, and later used.
- Notation table covers all recurring symbols.
- Each subproblem has model, solution, result, and validation.
- Results section answers the original questions directly.
- Sensitivity/robustness connects to model risk.
- Conclusion summarizes actionable findings and limitations.
