# CUMCM Figures Tables Checklist

- Every subproblem has at least one inspectable result artifact.
- Captions explain the artifact's meaning, not just its name.
- Axes, units, legends, and color meanings are clear.
- Tables use meaningful precision and units.
- Figures/tables are referenced from the text.
- Filenames are ASCII, descriptive, and stable.
- Avoid decorative plots that do not support an argument.
