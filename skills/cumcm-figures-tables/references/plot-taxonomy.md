# Plot Taxonomy

- Trend line: time series, forecasts, residuals.
- Scatter: relation between two variables, clusters, outliers.
- Bar chart: category comparison, ranking, ablation.
- Heatmap: matrix, correlation, spatial grid, sensitivity surface.
- Map or network: geography, flow, connectivity.
- Box/violin: distribution and variability.
- Pareto/frontier plot: multi-objective tradeoff.
- Flowchart: model pipeline or algorithm.

Choose the plot by the question it answers, not by visual appeal.
